Dr. Ravi Kiran Sarvadevabhatla is an Associate Professor affiliated with Centre for Visual Information Technology (CVIT) at IIIT Hyderabad since 2018. He is also the Lead for Mobility-related Applied Projects and Academic Programs at IHub-Data. Dr. Ravi Kiran obtained his Ph.D. from Indian Institute of Science (IISc) in 2018. His Ph.D. thesis is the recipient of IUPRAI Best Thesis Award and Sir Vital R Chandavarkar Memorial Gold Medal. Before joining IISc in 2014, he worked in US-based R&D companies during the period 2008-2013. He obtained his MS in Computer Science from University of Washington, Seattle, USA in 2008. Dr. Ravi Kiran has broad-ranging research interests and likes to work on inter-disciplinary problems involving multi-modal multimedia data (images, videos, text, audio) and disciplines (Graphics, Robotics, Human-Computer Interaction). For additional details, visit https://ravika.github.io/.
